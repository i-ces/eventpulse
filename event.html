Summer Music Festival 2025

Date & Time

Wednesday, January 15, 2025 • 6:00 PM - 11:00 PM

Location

Central Park Great Lawn, New York, NY 10024

Organizer

NYC Events Co.

About This Event

Join us for the ultimate summer music experience! The Summer Music Festival 2025 brings together world-renowned artists and emerging talents for an unforgettable evening of live performances under the stars.

Set against the stunning backdrop of Central Park's Great Lawn, this year's festival features an eclectic lineup spanning multiple genres including rock, pop, indie, and electronic music. With state-of-the-art sound systems, immersive light shows, and gourmet food vendors, this is more than just a concert—it's a celebration of music and community.

Whether you're a longtime festival-goer or attending your first outdoor concert, the Summer Music Festival promises an experience you won't forget. Bring your blankets, gather your friends, and get ready to dance the night away!
